Who we are what makes us go

iBeda Consultants is a full service market research company. We deliver insights by combining research outcomes; real time data and smart thinking to enable you make insightful decisions.

We have the flexibility of conducting Market research in East Africa and the rest of Africa. We understand the diversity that comes with each country.

Our capabilities include using of Qualitative and Quantitative techniques including Computer Assisted Telephone Interviews (CATI), Computer assisted Personal Interviews (CAPI), paper interview, mobile and online data collection, in-depth interviews and focus group discussions. We thrive in going beyond the obvious.

Over the last 12 years, iBeda Consultants has grown and has the ability to conduct research in most of Africa. Our awesome team of statisticians and psychologist with business acumen are committed to delivering insights and answering most business challenges. We are famous for our insight generation skills.

We are committed to engaging in long term partnerships that enable our clients benefit in a wholesome way to drive business success. At iBeda Consultants Ltd., We do not just give you data; we make your consumer come alive by assisting you see beyond the obvious.

    Research Tools what we use

    K-BUS

    This is a multi-client survey. It is a highly cost-effective way of understanding your target consumer while enjoying higher statistical reliability and flexibility

    K-Bus uses a representative sample of 1500 per quarter covering the whole of Kenya except North Eastern, Marsabit, Turkana and Samburu.

    K-Bus is designed to enable individuals, companies, public organizations, NGOs as well as religious organizations access research findings while optimizing on budgetary allocations

    K-Bus can be used for a one-off exploration and tracking of almost any topic of interest: brand awareness, advertising recall, attitudes, in-home presence, opinions on social issues

    Engage

    Engage is an ethnographic tool that bridges the gap between what people say they do and what they actually do.

    It allows companies to immerse themselves into their consumer’s lives in order to understand their world and real situations which in turn will translate into innovations, ideas and business opportunities.

    Engage allows for an understanding of needs that consumers cannot articulate without relying on consumer memory and recall by immersing into the life of the consumer.

    CUSTIX

    This is data analysis software that uses statistical methods to compute:

    • Satisfaction Indexes
    • Delight indexes
    • Frustration indexes
    Custix is able to achieve the following:
    • Provides segmentation of customers who are happy or frustrated with the specific issues contributing to the two segments
    • Guide to the attributes to be measured based on negative and positive touch points as well as service delivery standard attributes

    BASIX

    This is a tool that segments consumers with factors that drive their decision to use a brand or alternatives (competitors’ brand) into four segments:

    Characteristics are set / gathered through in-depth analysis using the qualitative technique which segments customer behavior towards a product/ service.

    These attributes are then measured and input on the tool to consolidates characteristics and give proportion to each segment. The tool is therefore good indicator to predict behavior shifts if one of the characteristics is altered in any given way.

    Pitch

    This is an advertisement test tool designed to evaluate promotional campaigns. It is developed to assist advertising agencies in optimizing their communication and marketing strategies and develop innovative media campaigns.

    Pitch 360 is designed to test all stages of advertising:

    Concept Refinement; It involves refining ideas/concepts into realistic consumer-oriented advertising concepts.

    Pre-test campaign; it involves testing an ad campaign before it is aired so as to enhance successful market adoption hence increasing awareness

    Post test campaign; It involves assessing the effect of promotional and marketing campaigns on the market.

    SME Explorer

    SME explorer is a iBeda Consultants Research product that entrust low budget research solutions to Small and Medium enterprises.

    Trendmasters

    Trendmasters Trendmasters is a iBeda Consultants’ Home-Grown tool that utilizes quick, fast and cost-effective Online/SMS survey solution allowing for top-of-mind consumer feedback and opinion polling, with results being delivered within 3 working days.

    Quantitative Research quantify problems

    Quantitative research quantifies problems by generating numerical data or data that can be transformed into usable statistics.

    It makes use of measurable data to formulate facts and uncover patterns. Quantitative research answers the questions how many, what is the ratio, what percentage, at what price and all other questions that need statistical validity. It is used to quantify attitudes opinions and behaviors.

    Our quantitative approaches include Face to Face interviews, Telephonic Interviews, Hall Tests, Consumer panels, Mystery shopping and Online surveys.


    Qualitative Research understand underlying reasons

    Qualitative research is used to understand underlying reasons, motivations and opinions, behaviors and social contexts. It is exploratory in nature and seeks to answer the questions why, how, what, when, where, and who.

    Our qualitative approaches include Focus Group Discussions, Mini Groups & Friendship Groups,Consumer Immersions,Triads, In-depth interviews, online group discussions, consumer panels, Creative Workshops and retail observations

    Creative workshops: We engage stakeholders in open discussions on research findings to generate deeper insights for implementation as strategy

    Consumer Immersions: We attempt to explain how culture represents the cultural constructions, in which we live through participant observations. Participants live and form bonds within the environment the ethnography is being conducted spending months or years within this environment.
